Welcome to RGU Art Society Hub!


Here you can JOIN our amazing Art Society!

We are the Art Society. You may ask yourself "What does Art Soc even do?"

Well, we do a lot of things. As the University does not offer courses in fine arts, we the students step in to bridge that gap. We offer chances to hone artistic skills, while also encouraging exploration of the arts locally in Aberdeenshire. We are most well known for our weekly life drawing class and our semesterly exhibitions.

“I love the Art Society, its full of life and beauty. I find the monthly magazines are something I look forward to and they keep me up to date with everything art!” Enara Abad RGU

“I can't imagine not being a part of the Art Society, my taste in art is eclectic and the Art Scoiety has helped me enjoy all areas of Art. ” Robbie Ewen RGU
